Artisan Coffee Roastery & Lounge

Cafe lounge's coffee is an award winning coffee roasting company established in Mullingar, Co. Westmeath.

We specialise in sourcing and roasting coffee for the premium commercial and speciality coffee sector. With over 30 years combined experience in coffee we are dedicated to the highest practices throughout our roasting process. Coffee Lounge is an amalgamation of a savvy business idea, passion for coffee and an experienced team.
