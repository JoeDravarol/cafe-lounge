Artisan Coffee Roastery & Lounge

Cafe lounge's coffee is an award winning coffee roasting company established in Mullingar, Co. Westmeath.

We specialise in sourcing and roasting coffee for the premium commercial and speciality coffee sector. With over 30 years combined experience in coffee we are dedicated to the highest practices throughout our roasting process. Cafe Lounge is an amalgamation of a savvy business idea, passion for coffee and an experienced team.

Cafe Lounge's Range

Using our experience to develop unique coffee blends with the optimum balance of acidity, body and flavour to make a great cup of coffee each time. All our raw coffee is sourced from ethical plantations around the coffee belt (north and south of the equator between the two tropics) through our coffee brokers.

Our unique method of hand roasting enables us to create a selection of balanced espresso blends varying in strength, body, flavour and aroma. Our success has led to winning numerous awards.

In 2014 we won a Three Star Gold for our Dragonfly Espresso, Two Star Gold for Dawn Chorus Blend, Two Star Gold for House Blend Espresso and a One Star Gold for Misty Mountain Filter in The Greater Taste Awards, in 2015 we won a Two Star Gold for our Dragonfly Espresso Blend and a One Star Gold for our Strictly Espresso Blend.
